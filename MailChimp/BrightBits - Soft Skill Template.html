Soft Skill Tip: Growth Mindset

 

Growth mindset, a term coined by psychologist Carol Dweck, refers to the belief that one’s intellectual ability can grow over time. This trait is especially crucial during times of change, such as when asking staff to embrace new strategies or integrate new technology. The more you understand about growth mindset, the more you can use it to inform your professional development plans.
