Embrace Professional Learning

 

As Ken Willers’ story illustrates, the value of providing teachers with choice in their PD is strong. Finding time to plan for personalization can feel daunting, but here’s a simple strategy to get you started creating experiences that will resonate with your teachers:

  • Distribute the Technology & Learning Professional Development Report to teacher-leaders and curriculum personnel. Have them think about what the data reveals about the staff’s current strengths and gaps.
     
  • As a group, select a few targeted learning experiences that address specific gaps, such as a session on using digital cameras as learning tools. Here's a resource to help.
     
  • Present the options you created to teachers and allow them to choose the learning experiences that are most relevant to their needs. Because you’ve selected options that address existing gaps, whatever they pick will help.
